The event was a fundraiser for Darlington County Schools to help fund SAT/ACT workshops, high school athletics, middle and high school backpacks, sixth-grade and first-grade composition books, third-grade dictionaries, Coats for Kids, a foster children Christmas party, Hartsville EdVenture and the Hartsville library.
