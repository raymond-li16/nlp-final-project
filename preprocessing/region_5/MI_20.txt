By RICK MILLS | rimills@medianewsgroup.com | The Morning Sun PUBLISHED: December 6, 2021 at 4:37 p.m. | UPDATED: December 6, 2021 at 5:19 p.m. An 11-year-old boy on his bicycle was killed Monday afternoon when he went around a semi parked in front of his house and was hit by an oncoming vehicle, state police said. Troopers from the Lakeview Post are investigating the crash that occurred just before 1 p.m. on East McBride’s Road near North Douglas Road in Ferris Township, Montcalm County. Ferris Township is south of Vestaburg and north of Crystal. “Initial investigation reveals that an 11-year-old boy rode his bike around the front of a semi, which was parked in front of his residence, and rode directly into the path of an eastbound vehicle driven by a 34-year-old female from Carson City,” police said.
