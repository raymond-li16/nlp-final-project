Published by admin on Thu, 06/24/2021 - 9:51am Attendees at this year’s Taste in Cumberland enjoyed an evening of good food, friends & fun on Monday, June 21st at the Cumberland Golf Club. The annual event benefits the Boys & Girls Clubs of Barron County’s Cumberland Extension and features “the newest & finest products from regional eating establishments, breweries, distilleries & wineries.” A great time was had by all!
