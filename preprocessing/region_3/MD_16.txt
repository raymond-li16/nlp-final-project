Dundalk Community Theatre to perform "Winter Wonderettes" at CCBC Dec 2, 2021  Facebook Twitter Email Print Save Dundalk Community Theatre, in residence at the Community College of Baltimore County, will open the 2021-2022 season with Winter Wonderettes, a 1960s holiday celebration originally created by Roger Bean. The show will be held in the John E. Ravekes Theatre at the College Community Center, CCBC Dundalk, 7200 Sollers Point Road. Performances will be held at 3 p.m. and 8 p.m. on Saturday, Dec. 11, and 3 p.m. on Sunday, Dec. 12. According to a press release from CCBC, the show goes back in time and joins the Wonderettes as they save the annual holiday party when Santa is a no show, and features ’60s versions of holiday classics such as Santa Claus Is Comin’ to Town, Jingle Bell Rock, Run Rudolph Run, Winter Wonderland and more. Dickie Mahoney will direct the Dundalk Community Theatre cast of actors featuring Emily Machovec, Stephanie Mahoney, Becca Vourvoulas Szymanski and Katie Gardner. Ticket prices are $22 for adults, $20 for seniors and $15 for students and children. They are available at the CCBC Box Office at 443-840-ARTS (2787) or online at ccbctickets.com. Seating is limited and arranged to ensure social distancing; masks are required.
