Parking Ban in Effect in Peshtigo
Issue Date: December 3, 2021
Peshtigo City Police Chief, Fred Popp, reminds residents of the winter parking ban. All night parking is prohibited unless a permit has been issued by the Police Department. It shall be unlawful to park or leave unattended any automobile, truck or other motor vehicle between the hours of 1 a.m. and 8 a.m. between Dec. 1, 2021 and March 31, 2022, both dates inclusive, on the following streets in Peshtigo.
