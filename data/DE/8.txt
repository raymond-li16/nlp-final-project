A 40-year-old woman is in stable condition after being shot in Wilmington's Browntown section of the city. 

According to Wilmington Police, the victim was shot in the 700 block of Maryland Avenue around 3:50 p.m. on Sunday, December 5, 2021. 

This was all the information made immediately available by police. 

Anyone with information regarding this incident is urged to contact Wilmington Police Det. Danielle Moore at 302.576.3667 or Delaware Crime Stoppers at 1.800.TIP.3333.
