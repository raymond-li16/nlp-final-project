2 seriously injured in Wasilla vehicle collision
By Anchorage Daily News
Updated: 26 minutes ago Published: 4 hours ago
Two people sustained life-threatening injuries in a Wasilla vehicle collision Thursday afternoon, Alaska State Troopers said.

Around 1:46 p.m. Tuesday, Palmer troopers responded to West Sunset Avenue and Gon Fishin Drive for a report of a vehicle crash, troopers said in an online report. The two-vehicle collision resulted in multiple injuries, and the two individuals were being taken to the hospital, according to troopers.

Troopers said around 2:40 p.m. that the roadways would be closed temporarily as they worked to document the scene using a drone.
