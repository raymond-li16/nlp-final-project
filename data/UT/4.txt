

By Patsy Stoddard

The River Runners Hall of Fame at the John Wesley Powell River History museum hosted the induction of three new members. There was a banquet and a presentation honoring their achievements. George Wendt, Kenny Ross and Gaylord Stavley have long been icons in the river running business. There information will be on display at the museum with a history of their river running years and the improvements they helped bring to the industry.
