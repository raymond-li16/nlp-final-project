Piper’s Opera House hosts ‘A Comstock Christmas Carol’
‘A Comstock Christmas Carol’ in 2019. (Photo: Melody Hoover)

The historic Piper’s Opera House in Virginia City, hosts “A Comstock Christmas Carol” this holiday season. The classic story with a Comstock twist is set to be performed by the Piper’s Players with performances in December.
The family-friendly play features the story of the Christmas Carol and is set to the fun and light-hearted music of the Muppets Christmas Carol.
The classic Christmas tale is one for the whole family with music and dancing to entertain all ages. Shows take place Dec. 10 at 7 p.m., Dec. 11 at 2 p.m. and 7 p.m., and Dec. 12 at 2 p.m. Tickets are $30 for adults and $20 for children ages 4-12.
Buy tickets at https://www.eventbrite.com/e/a-comstock-christmas-carol-tickets-191869917087. For information, visit PipersOperaHouse.com or call 775-847-0433
