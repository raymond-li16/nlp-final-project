Brush off your best work from this year and enter it in the 2021 VPA News and Advertising Competition
 Open Audio Article Player
December 03, 2021

FacebookTwitterEmailMessengerCopy LinkSMSShare
The VPA contest site will be open through Jan. 24, 2022
It’s time to dig up your best writing, photo, advertising, digital and graphics work to enter into the 2021 VPA News & Advertising Contest.

Compete with your counterparts from around the state in one of the largest press association contests in the country. The contest is judged by peers from another state press association.

Designated contest contacts at each member newspaper should have received details and rules on Tuesday, Nov. 30 from Janet Madison, VPA awards contest administrator. The information is also available on the VPA website here.
